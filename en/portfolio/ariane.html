Ariane

What is Ariane?

Ariane is a website created by the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs. It allows French people to list their trips or occasional missions abroad. Totally free of charge, it also enables travelers to receive safety advice and be informed of possible risks in their destination country.

This service is useful and can prove to be vital in some cases, yet it is little-known due to a lack of communication and ergonomics of the platform.

What is Ariane?

Ariane is a website created by the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs. It allows French people to list their trips or occasional missions abroad. Totally free of charge, it also enables travelers to receive safety advice and be informed of possible risks in their destination country.

This service is useful and can prove to be vital in some cases, yet it is little-known due to a lack of communication and ergonomics of the platform.

How to improve it?

We can use tourism as a means of collecting data, which will be transmitted to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs throught the mobile application. The app geolocates French citizens and warns them of the risks incurred in the countries concerned, listing all the necessary information and contacts in case of a problem.

The target is very broad, since according to STD (Suivi de la Demande Touristique) surveys in 2016, 26 millions trips were made by French people abroad (personal and professional reasons combined).

Social network function

A function only dedicated to the users and encouraging them to exchange around 4 themes: outdoor activities, transport, restaurant, tourism.

Inside of each of these sections, the user will find a place to exchange. This option was created in order for the French community to help each other.

Gamification of the app

A content with some point of touristic interests to visit will be available. Then, if they want, users can add content by integrating in the app a new point of interest and could even create playful courses rated by the community.

A difficulty level added to a description is set up for each new playful courses created by a user. A user could level up after having visited other points of interest of another user. A ranking system will be available for the users in the geographical range concerned.
