Orphée

“Let your music inspiration speak for itself wherever you are”

Problem description

There are some mobile applications that allow the user to compose music directly on the phone or tablet. But the problem is that they are not fluent/easy to use for creative people and they can't transfer the user's composition file to the music editing software on computer.

Application main functions

  1. Making electronic music on a smartphone
  2. Exporting the composition files to music editing software (if one modification is made on one device, it will automatically do it on the second one)
  3. Sharing the composition with another producer or on platforms
  4. Cloud service

View Site Mapping

Potential roles

  • People who wants to make music on their phone
  • People who wants to express their creativity wherever they are
  • People who wants to work on their music in a more efficient way
  • People who wants to share their work

Targeted user

The user is a professional music producer who is making music for a living. He is a tech-addict, always looking for some application to improve his way of life. He is also someone dedicated to his work, someone really passionate and straight-forward. He is therefore looking for a functional music application that permits him to be the most efficient possible when he is working on his productions.
