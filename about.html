It's a pleasure to meet you! I am French and currently based in Nantes, France. I have just graduated with a Master in Digital Management with a focus on User Experience and Web Designing. As a UX/UI Designer my purpose is to deliver the best experience possible to the user whether it is on mobile, tablet or desktop.

I have worked for an advertising company of a French TV Channel called M6 based in Paris as a "Marketing Assistant". And for an e-commerce company iSi GmbH based in Darmsdadt, Germany as a "UX/UI Designer & A/B Testing Analyst" where I was in charge of their 4 websites (France, UK, Netherlands and Italy).

Therefore, my understanding for the business & marketing part coupled with my designing abilities for the user experience and user interface are skills that a project can benefit from in a positive and constructive way.
