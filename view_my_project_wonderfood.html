Wonderfood

“We are a wonderfood team dedicated to give people the best delicious break possible!”

Restaurant concept

The restaurant is located in Bordeaux, France in Victoire’s place which is the center of the city. Its customers are located near the city center.


The concept is to deliver food cooked with fare trade ingredients where the client is exactly located thanks to his phone.

Stakeholders needs

  • User needs
    • Find out if the restaurant delivers to their exact location
    • Order food online
    • Being able to personalize the cheesecakes
    • Contact the team for special events
    • Able to reserve food on a day at a specific time

  • Client needs
    • To sell food online that will be delivered
    • Provide a system for order customization
    • Funny interactions for the brand concept
    • System to track the delivery
    • Chatbot in order for the customer to contact the company

Outline of scope
